Departments in the college

Electrical Department

The Department of Electrical Engineering in Shubra: A distinguished educational center that aims to prepare competent electrical engineers.
The department offers modern curricula covering the fields of energy, electronics, communications, and control.
It features a specialized academic staff and well-equipped laboratories that provide practical experience.
It is committed to training students and developing their skills to keep pace with the job market.
Join us and be part of the future of electrical engineering!

Mechanics Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering in Shubra is a distinguished educational center that aims to prepare competent and innovative mechanical engineers.
The department offers modern curricula covering design, production, energy, thermal engineering, and other fields.
It has well-equipped laboratories and workshops to provide students with the necessary practical experience.
It is committed to developing students' research and applied skills through diverse projects and activities.
The department strives to graduate leaders capable of meeting industrial needs and contributing to development.

Communications and Computers Department

The Department of Communications and Computer Science at Shubra University Faculty of Engineering keeps pace with the latest technologies in the fields of communications, computer networks, software engineering, and artificial intelligence. It aims to prepare specialized engineers capable of designing, developing, and maintaining smart systems and modern communication networks.
The department offers advanced laboratories and modern curricula that focus on practical and applied aspects.
It contributes to meeting the growing labor market needs in the communications and information technology sectors.
Join us to be at the forefront of technological development!
